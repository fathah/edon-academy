About Edon Academy

Edon Academy is a learning institution based in Kerala. We have been effectively educating students with highly skilled subject experts both digitally and practically. Students from grades 1 through 7 can enroll in Edon Academy. We offer extracurricular activities together with academic courses. We combine the benefits of a traditional school with the flexibility of individual support and personalized plans. Our unique and flexible learning model allows students to study at home, wherever they are. Our adaptable programs are designed and structured to help expat students, especially those residing in GCC countries, to pursue their education at home.
Edon Academy provides a fully digital, well organized curriculum for students including live class sessions, and collaborative environments while giving teachers flexibility to maximize students' full potential. Edon Academy offers virtual clubs to all students, as well as student meetups on a regional basis.
